# Prep flow

Pre-meeting brief for one stakeholder.

Invocation: `/darin prep rahul` or `/darin prep Talia Brennan`

## Step 1: Load

- `stakeholders/<slug>.md` (create stub from interview if missing)
- `STRATEGY.md`
- Hypotheses they care about or have veto on
- Recent `ingestion/` mentioning them

## Step 2: Output (one page)

```markdown
# Prep: [Name] — [meeting type if known]

## Their open asks
## Last unresolved concern
## Evidence they tend to ignore (cite files)
## Suggested questions (3–5)
## Landmines (topics that derail)
## Your decision goal for this meeting
```

## Tone

Darin prepares the PM to run the meeting, not to win an argument. Evidence citations required when contradicting a stakeholder claim.
