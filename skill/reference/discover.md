# Discover flow

Plan and run a customer discovery interview, then route synthesis through ingest.

## Step 1: Frame the learning goal

Ask (if not in invocation):

- What decision does this interview inform?
- Who is the ideal participant?
- What would falsify your current belief?

## Step 2: Interview plan

Output:

- 5–8 questions (Mom Test compliant: past behavior, not hypotheticals)
- Screener criteria for participant
- Note-taking template for live capture
- What **not** to ask (solution pitching, "would you use")

## Step 3: After the interview

Direct user to `/darin ingest <transcript>` or ingest yourself if transcript is provided.

Do not skip ingest. Discovery without memory is waste.

## Step 4: Suggest follow-up

Based on gaps: another interview segment, `shape`, or `plan`.
