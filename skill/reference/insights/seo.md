# SEO recipe

Source-level messaging in meta tags, titles, and headings тАФ not a technical crawl audit.

## What to find

`layout.tsx`, `metadata` exports, `<Head>`, `sitemap`, `robots`, page-level title/description.

## Recipe-specific checks

1. **Title messaging** тАФ does `<title>` reflect positioning vs generic product name?
2. **Meta description** тАФ specific pain/outcome or boilerplate?
3. **H1 alignment** тАФ does the primary heading match title/meta and `PRODUCT.md` Users?
4. **Page differentiation** тАФ pricing vs landing vs docs titles distinct and accurate?
5. **Open Graph** тАФ if present, does social preview copy match what you want shared?

## Common misalignments

- Every page title is just the product name
- Meta description targets wrong audience vs landing copy
- H1 says "Welcome" while strategy says you own a specific niche
