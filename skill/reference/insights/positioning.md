# Positioning nudge

**PM question:** Do outward surfaces clearly say who it's for, what problem we solve, and why trust us?

## How to check

Walk outward-facing surfaces as the buyer in memory — marketing pages, meta copy, footers, trust content. Find files yourself.

## What to look for

- Headline and hero vs `PRODUCT.md` Users and Problem
- Problem clarity vs evidence themes in ingestion
- Trust and proof gaps (derive specifics from memory — security review, social proof, credibility blockers)
- Primary CTA vs how they evaluate
- Emphasis vs `STRATEGY.md` current focus — sprawl or sharp story?
