# Landing recipe

## What to find

Marketing homepage, hero, value prop, primary CTA, social proof blocks.

## Recipe-specific checks

1. **Hero ICP** — who does the headline speak to? Match against `PRODUCT.md` Users.
2. **Problem clarity** — is the pain specific within 5 seconds, or generic ("streamline workflows")?
3. **CTA intent** — does the primary button match your stage (signup vs demo vs waitlist)?
4. **Social proof** — logos, quotes, metrics present? If interviews cite trust as a blocker, is it addressed?
5. **Focus vs. sprawl** — does the page emphasize your current `STRATEGY.md` focus, or list every feature?

## Common misalignments

- Speaks to developers when primary user is ops/business
- Leads with features when customers care about outcomes
- Promises something in `What you're not doing`
