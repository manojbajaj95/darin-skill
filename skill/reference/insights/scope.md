# Scope nudge

**PM question:** Is shipped surface area focused vs strategy — or jargon-heavy sprawl that hides the aha?

## How to check

Inventory in-product and outward surface area: nav, feature lists, step counts, terminology density. Compare to `STRATEGY.md` focus and exclusions.

## What to look for

- Feature sprawl vs `What you're focused on` and `What you're not doing`
- Jargon vs `PRODUCT.md` Users (non-technical buyers lost in technical language)
- Too many paths before aha when memory emphasizes speed to value
- Surfaces that sell or expose capabilities strategy deprioritizes
- Bloat vs missing — over-emphasis, not absence
